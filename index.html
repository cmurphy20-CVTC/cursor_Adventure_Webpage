Survive the Fallout.

The world has ended, all of the world’s superpowers have crumbled and there’s little humanity remaining. This occurred in the year 2099. A viral disease wiped out a great amount of people and amidst the pandemic people that maintained many nuclear silos left them unattended to. This resulted in their instability and eventual destruction resulting in the world you will find out today. Before this all happened your great grandparents warned you this very well could happen. They lived through something similar over 50 years ago. Somehow they knew something like this would happen again. Taking their insight you had a bomb shelter made and stocked full of supplies in case this happened. On the fateful day of November 26th 2099 all of the unattended nuclear bombs went off all over the planet and ravaged the countries. It’s time for you to reemerge into the ravaged world to seek out food and water.
